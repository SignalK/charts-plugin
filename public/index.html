Using the Chart Seeding Addon

  1. Click on the map to draw a region to seed charts for.
  2. In the region dialog, select chart provider, zoom levels and options.
  3. Click "Create Seeding Job" to add the job to the job list.
  4. Open the job list by clicking on the "Jobs" button to monitor progress.
  5. You can start/pause or remove jobs from the job list.
